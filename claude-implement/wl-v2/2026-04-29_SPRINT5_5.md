### STEP 4: Approval queue + Subscription requests
File: src/core/auth/company-approval.service.ts + controller
  GET    /companies/:companyId/pending-members              → List PENDING mappings
  POST   /companies/:companyId/members/:id/approve          → Approve (→ ACTIVE)
  POST   /companies/:companyId/members/:id/reject           → Reject (→ REJECTED + reason)
  POST   /companies/:companyId/subscription-requests        → Request Company→Company sub
  GET    /companies/:companyId/subscription-requests        → List pending sub requests
  POST   /companies/:companyId/subscription-requests/:id/approve
  POST   /companies/:companyId/subscription-requests/:id/reject

Subscription rules:
- Invited by company → AUTO-APPROVE (joinMode=INVITED, status=ACTIVE)
- Individual → Company → AUTO-APPROVE (requiresApproval=false)
- Company → Company → REQUIRES APPROVAL (CompanySubscriptionRequest flow)
